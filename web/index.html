Apply for a Standard Visitor visa: step by step

Check what you need to do before you can apply for a Standard Visitor visa and what to do when you arrive in the UK.

  1. Step 1 Check if you need a visa

    1. Check the Standard Visitor visa is right for you

  2. Step 2 Prepare the evidence you need

    1. Check what you need to show evidence of
    2. Check what documents you can use as evidence
    3. Check what to do if you're under 18
    4. If your visa is for more than 6 months, check if you need a TB test

  3. Step 3 Apply

    1. Apply for a visa and book an appointment £93 to £798

    When you apply you need to book an appointment at a visa application centre.

    1. Check what country your nearest visa application centre is in

    You should get a decision within 3 weeks of your appointment.

    You can pay an extra £212 for a decision within 5 days or £956 for a decision within 24 hours.

  4. Step 4 Attend an appointment

    1. What you need to show at your appointment

    You'll be told when a decision has been made about your visa.

  5. Step 5 Travel to the UK

    1. Check what you need to show at the border
    2. Check what you can bring with you

    You must leave the UK on the date specified on your visa. If you stay longer you may not be able to return to the UK again.
